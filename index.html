About the app

Jamatron takes listening parties to a whole new level. Jam to your favorite songs or to a fresh out the oven release from one of your favorite artists with this innovative new social music service.

Jamatron connects to your everyday-use streaming service provider and allows you and your friends to jam together, in real time, to the same music, from a distance.

Have you ever wanted to stream music along with your friends from a distance, but you use different streaming services? Jamatron comes as a bridge over that problem! With Jamatron, you are able to connect various streaming services and create rooms for you and other people to join and play the exact same song at the exact same time! Easy, with no buffering, right to your app of choice.

While some services such as Spotify do offer some sort of streaming session feature, it is kept under a monthly paywall and it is exclusive to the users of that service. With Jamatron, the barriers are broken.

As a one-time purchase, the Jamatron app offers you seamless integration with numerous streaming apps and the ability to create unlimited rooms.

As for the Official Streaming Parties, we are still thinking of ways of giving access to people. We are considering selling entry tickets or making it subscription-based.
